Sushi



Sushi is the most simple dish on this website. The quality mostly comes from the quality of the fish. Apart from that, you are just making some sushi rice with a bit of vinegar, shaping it with your palm, and putting the sliced fish on tiop with a bit of wasabi.


Ingredients:

  • Sashimi grade fish
  • Sushi rice
  • Wasabi
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Cook the sushi rice with a small bit of vinegar, as well as sea salt or kombu if desired
  2. Slice the fish thinly using a sharp knife. Fish should be sliced slightly at an angle.
  3. Rub a small dash of wasabi on the underside of your fish using your finger
  4. Shape rice with hand, and place fish on rice
