Gyoza



Making gyoza is a lot easier than ramen. all you need to do is mix meat, green onion and spices into a bowl, and then spoon the mixture into the gyoza skins. After that you just need to fold the gyoza skins over on themselves and score the edges together with your fingers


Ingredients:

  • Ground pork
  • Green onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Gyoza Skins

Steps:

  1. Mix the meat, green onion, and spices together
  2. Spoon mixture into gyoza skins
  3. Fold gyoza skins into dumplings, scoring the edges with your fingers and a bit of water
